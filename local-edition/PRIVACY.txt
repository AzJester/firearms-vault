SUMMARY
This app is built to keep your information private. Everything you enter stays
on your own device. We do not run a server for this app, we do not have an
account system, and we never receive, see, store, or sell your data.

WHAT WE COLLECT
Nothing. The app has no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, and no user
accounts. We collect zero personal information through the app itself.

WHERE YOUR DATA LIVES
All of your records, photos, and documents are stored locally in your browser's
on-device storage (IndexedDB) on the device where you use the app. Backups you
create with "Save to File" or "Backup Now" are ordinary files saved wherever
you choose; you control them entirely.

THIRD-PARTY CODE LIBRARIES
To work, the app loads some open-source code libraries (for charts, spreadsheet
and PDF export, QR codes, and optional serial-number scanning). Depending on how
your copy is installed, these may be requested from a public CDN the first time
you open the app. Such a request is a normal web request to the library host and
contains no data from your collection; it only fetches the library code. If your
copy bundles these libraries locally, no such request is made. Your collection
data is never sent anywhere.

YOUR RESPONSIBILITY
Because your data is stored only on your device, protecting and backing it up is
up to you. Anyone with access to your unlocked device may be able to open the
app. Use your device's security features, and consider the optional local file
encryption in Settings for exported files.

CHILDREN
This app is intended for adults and is not directed to children.

CHANGES
If this policy changes, the updated version will ship with future releases.
